# INCLUSIVE EDUCATION FRAMEWORK

## 1. Guiding Model: UDL (Universal Design for Learning)
Platform-wide implementation:
1. Multiple means of engagement
2. Multiple means of representation
3. Multiple means of action/expression

## 2. Gifted Learners (دانش‌آموزان تیزهوش)
- Acceleration diagnostics and compacting
- Enrichment pathways and independent inquiry projects
- Optional advanced problem banks and mentorship referrals

## 3. ADHD-Specific Design
- Micro-content chunks (<=5 minutes when possible)
- Frequent interaction checkpoints
- Visual timers and optional movement break prompts
- Reduced-distraction mode (minimal UI noise)
- Immediate, clear reinforcement feedback

## 4. Learning Disability Accommodations
- Text-to-speech and speech-to-text
- Font, spacing, contrast, and color controls
- Simplified language overlays
- Multi-modal explanations (audio/visual/interactive)

## 5. ELL / Multilingual Support
- Bilingual glossary and just-in-time vocabulary support
- Concept previews in preferred language
- Scaffolding sentence starters for writing/discussion

## 6. Scaffolding Engine
- Optional hints (layered)
- Worked examples and step reveal
- Adaptive support intensity based on attempt history

## 7. Assessment Inclusion
- Extended-time settings (where policy allows)
- Alternate response modes (oral/video/text)
- Rubrics sensitive to language-development stage

## 8. Research-Informed Practices (Applied)
- Cognitive load management via chunking
- Retrieval practice and spaced review
- Metacognitive reflection prompts
- Belonging and self-efficacy supports

## 9. Implementation Checklist
- Accessibility QA on all templates
- Modality minimum: every major concept in >=3 forms
- Inclusion audit before publication
