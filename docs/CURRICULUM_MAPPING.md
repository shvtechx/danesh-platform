# CURRICULUM MAPPING

## 1. Iranian Curriculum (KG–12) Overview

### KG (پیش‌دبستانی)
Language readiness, early numeracy, motor skills, socio-emotional foundations.

### Primary (دبستان, 1–6)
Core subjects typically include: Persian literacy, mathematics, science, social studies, Quran/religious studies, arts, physical education, and introductory English (as applicable by policy).

### Lower Secondary (راهنمایی / متوسطه اول, 7–9)
Persian language/literature, Arabic, English, mathematics, science, social studies, religious studies, culture/arts, technology/work skills, PE.

### Upper Secondary (دبیرستان / متوسطه دوم, 10–12)
Track-based specialization:
- ریاضی فیزیک: advanced math, physics, chemistry
- تجربی: biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics
- انسانی: literature, history, sociology, philosophy/logic
- علوم و معارف اسلامی: Islamic studies-focused curriculum
- فنی حرفه‌ای / کار و دانش: technical and applied vocational pathways

## 2. International Framework Overview
- **IB:** PYP (inquiry transdisciplinary), MYP (concept-based), DP (specialized + TOK/EE/CAS)
- **US Common Core:** ELA/Math standards + state additions/NGSS style science alignment
- **British National Curriculum:** Key Stages and subject attainment targets
